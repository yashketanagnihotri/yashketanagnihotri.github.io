Present Situation

Rural education in india has always been a problem since indepence. It is one of the biggest hurdle in the growing economy of the country .the destitute people do not have enough money to attend schools and the schools located in backward areas are also not equipped with enough support to help the child grow .

Our Concept

We at Gadget Library are like a Library where you can borrow various technological gadgets which will be helpful in various forms of education in the Rural areas where it is very difficult to get a quality education infact most people dont even have a smartphone so we will be providing various gadgets and some of them are mentioned below :-

1.Laptops : We will be providing laptops with all the basic functionality of a basic laptop and will be provided with most online learning platforms like IGNOU,NPTEL,COURSERA(with 200 free courses) and much more

2.Tablets : This is mainly targeted for children less that fourteen years of age,this tablet will be equipped with a lot of educational videos and some cartoons for small children to keep their intrest in studies.We feel that tablets can really clear up the concepts for the lower classes so that they can be more comoftable in higher classes.

Internet Dongles : We will also provide a dongle for providing Internet to the families with no Internet or limited connnectivity.We will be providing 2GB of Internet services and we will be monitoring the websites which are being used and all Vulgar and explicite websites will be banned and wont be accessible.
